VPS Hosting

VPS hosting has recently been the most popular hosting service. It comes with inborn flexibility and security but costs lower than a dedicated server. If you want to take full advantage of computing resources and have the ability to manage the operating system, VPS is the best choice. Our VPSs are characterized by super-fast performance, hard security, cost efficiency, and pure/hybrid SSD solutions.

Dedicated Server Hosting

In most cases, dedicated server hosting is a better solution than cloud hosting. Our dedicated servers are pure bare metals without any overhead of the virtualization layer, and with full IPMI direct access. This offers expert clients the highest flexibility and advanced management interface. We have a variety of dedicated server configurations from simple commodity servers to the state-of-the-art server clusters. Moreover, we support on-demand configuration to accommodate clients’ various requirements.
